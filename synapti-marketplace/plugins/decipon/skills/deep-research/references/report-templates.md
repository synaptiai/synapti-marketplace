## Standard Research Report

```markdown
# [Descriptive Title]

## Executive Summary
[2-3 paragraphs: main question, key findings, recommendation]

## Background
[Context: why this matters, current state, key definitions]

## Methodology
[Sources consulted, approach, limitations]

## Findings

### [Key Question 1]
[Findings with citations]

### [Key Question 2]
[Continue for each question]

## Analysis
[Synthesis: patterns, implications, consensus/disagreement]

## Limitations
[Gaps, uncertainties, future research needed]

## Conclusion
[Final synthesis and recommendations]

## Sources
[Numbered list with title, author, date, URL]
```

## Formatting Guidelines

### Citations
- Inline: "According to [Source], [fact]"
- Parenthetical: "[Fact] ([Source])"

### Confidence Language
- **High**: "X is", "Research shows", "Evidence demonstrates"
- **Medium**: "X appears to be", "Findings suggest"
- **Low**: "X may be", "Some sources suggest"

### Length Guidelines
- Executive Summary: 150-300 words
- Major sections: 200-500 words
- Total: 1,500-5,000 words (scale to complexity)
